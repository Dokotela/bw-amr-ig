Resource Profile: Botswana AMR Specimen

Official URL: http://bw.health.gov/fhir/ImplementationGuide/bw-amr-ig/StructureDefinition/botswana-amr-specimen Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2025-07-08 Computable Name: BotswanaAMRSpecimen

Specimen profile for Botswana AMR surveillance with required collection details

Usages:

  • Refer to this Profile: Botswana AMR DiagnosticReport, Botswana AMR Gram Stain Observation, Botswana AMR Organism Identification Observation, Botswana AMR Special Test Observation and Botswana AMR Susceptibility Observation
